Introduction

We are Multimedia & Artifical Intelligence Security laboratory from the School of Computer Science in Fudan University.

The laboratory consists of three faculties (Zhang Xinpeng, Qian Zhenxing, Li Sheng), some postdoctoral fellows, and many Ph.D. & master students. MAS lab focuses on multimedia and artificial intelligence security, including topics of information hiding, multimedia forensics, artificial intelligence and multimedia applications. MAS lab has published over 400 academic papers many of which are published in the journals and conferences like IEEE TPAMI, TIFS, TIP, TDSC, TCSVT, TMM, TCYB, ICCV, NeurIPS, AAAI, IJCAI, and ACM MM.

Feel free to follow and support Fudan MAS Laboratory!
