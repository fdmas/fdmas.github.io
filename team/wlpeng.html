Postdoctoral Research Fellow

School of Computer Science, Fudan University

E-mail: pengwanli@fudan.edu.cn

I received the ph.D. Degree from the school of Information and Electric Engineering, China Agricultural University, Beijing, China, in 2022, under the supervision of Prof. Yiming Xue. I am currently a Postdoctoral Research Fellow with the School of Computer Science, Fudan University, China. My research interests include information hiding and artificial intelligence security.
