Multimedia Forensics

The group focuses on three main topics: passive image forensics, proactive image forensics, and fake news detection. Passive image forensics include image manipulation detection, DeepFake detection, model fingerprinting, etc. For proactive image forensics, we utilize data hiding, our lab’s key strength, to assist forgery detection.
